    About BSit


    BSit is short term for the word "baby sit" whereas it is related to the webpage's service. BSit is also related to the program taken by the developers of the webpage, which is BS Information Technology. BSit is a free website application where parents will be able to find nannies for their children and nannies will be able to find parents who need them. This application provides an easier and faster process of booking a nanny with the use of modern technology. With the use of BSit, we can help parents who nned someone to take care of their children, and help nannies gain income to sustain their daily needs.
